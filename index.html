Recipe for Paella

Paella Like the Pros

Prep Time
45 Mins
cook time
45 Mins

Ingredients

  • Bomba Rice or Calespera Rice
  • Shrimp
  • Saffron
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Clams
  • Chicken Thigh or Rabbit
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Flat Bottom Pan or Paella Pan
  • Broth Or Stock
  • Paprika
  • Garlic

Spanish people get very upset when people do not make authentica Paella Authentic Paella consists of Calespera rice, Rabbit, Mussels, Saffron, and a sofrito to bind. This is specfically known as a Paella Valencia where the dish originates from. This version can be as authentic or inauthetic as you desire. Just remember to cook with a beer or wine for personal consumption.

Getting Started

WE HAVE THE MEATS
  1. First, heat selected pan with olive oil, and heat stock in a small sauce pan.
  2. Sautee onions and peppers until translucent, Add garlic and stir not to burn garlic.
  3. Add chicken thighs or rabbit, fry till golden brown.
  4. Add tomato paste and stir to make a sofrito.
  5. Toast rice in pan till golden and continue stirring so not to burn.
  6. Use shrimp shells in stock to flavor the broth further. Pour liquid in to the mixture in the pan
  7. Bring to a boil and add shrimp and shellfish to cook. Shake pan to settle rice.
  8. Season with seasonings from high
  9. Let simmer and wait for water to evaporate
  10. Garnish with Lemon
